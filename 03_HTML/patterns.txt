General patterns:

Normal ten digits phone pattern: "[0-9]{10}"

phone number with country code: "[+91 ]{4}[0-9]{10}"

Username patter: "[A-Z]{1}[a-z]{1,}[0-9]{1,}"

Password: 

1. Password with 8 characters: [A-Za-z0-9@#$%^]{8}

2. First letter capital, includes small letters, then @ symbol,

 then three digits: Amar@123 -- [A-Z]{1}[a-z]+[@]{1}[0-9]{3}

Email pattern: [a-z]+[@]{1}[a-z0-9]+[.com]{4}

Audio tag in HTML:

src - address of audio 

type - mp3,ogg

controls - interface

autoplay - if we want to play audio automatically when someone open your web site.

loop - play song continously

muted - by-default muted audio
